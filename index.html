Who am I

Open Science and GIS lover, maps developer and apprentice researcher in oceanography. Currently working at CNR-IRBIM in Ancona (Italy) and studying the impact on fish communities of artificial and offshore structures. Always pursuing the principles of Open Science in research and day-by-day fighting for their application.
